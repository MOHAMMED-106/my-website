Importance of Cybersecurity

The importance of cybersecurity lies in protecting information and systems from cyberattacks that may lead to data theft, service disruptions, or system damage. Cybersecurity includes maintaining the confidentiality of data, protecting it from unauthorized access, ensuring its integrity, and ensuring its availability when needed. It also helps in:

  • Protecting personal data: Safeguarding individuals' information from theft or leakage.
  • Preventing cyberattacks: Blocking attempts to hack or launch malicious attacks.
  • Ensuring business continuity: Keeping operations running smoothly without negative effects from cyberattacks.
  • Protecting digital assets: Securing information and systems relied upon by companies and governments.
