What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity involves protecting systems, networks, and data from malicious attacks. It ensures the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of sensitive information.

Key Principles of Cybersecurity

Confidentiality
Ensures that data is accessible only to authorized individuals.
Integrity
Ensures that information is not altered in an unauthorized manner.
Availability
Ensures that systems and information are available to authorized users when needed.
